About us

WatchWorks, a company specializing in the sale and servicing of watches, was founded on April 22, 1932, by Jimmy Black along with his brothers Raylen and Robert. However, it was Jimmy who made the most significant contributions, dedicating considerable effort and financial resources, particularly in Johnstown, United Kingdom. Through meticulous craftsmanship and a passion for horology, WatchWorks quickly gained recognition for its precision and quality in both watch sales and repair services.

Over the years, WatchWorks has established itself as a trusted name in the watch industry, earning a loyal customer base and expanding its reach to cater to enthusiasts worldwide. Despite the evolving trends and technological advancements, WatchWorks remains committed to upholding its legacy of excellence, blending traditional expertise with modern innovation.

With a team of skilled watchmakers and a commitment to customer satisfaction, WatchWorks continues to thrive, offering a diverse range of timepieces from renowned brands and providing expert repair and maintenance services. Today, WatchWorks stands as a testament to Jimmy Black's vision and dedication, carrying forward a heritage of timeless craftsmanship and precision in every tick of the clock.

Today, the watch store is successfully managed by Richard Black, the heir of the Black lineage, maintaining the legacy of excellence established by Jimmy Black. Under Richard's leadership, the store continues to thrive, catering to a diverse clientele and upholding the tradition of timeless craftsmanship and impeccable service.
